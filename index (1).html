About Miden

What is Miden?

Miden catalyzes onchain finance by combining programmable privacy, quantum-resistance, and unrivaled scalability — all in one network.

Through pioneering ZK (Zero-Knowledge) architecture, Miden enables local and private transaction execution. Users and builders can choose between fully public or completely private transactions, creating a new class of compliant financial applications that were previously impossible to build onchain. Backed by top-tier investors including a16z Crypto, 1kx, Hack VC, P2 Ventures, and more, Miden is designed to be the infrastructure layer for the future of finance.
